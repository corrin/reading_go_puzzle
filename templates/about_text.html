About Tsumego Solver

Tsumego Solver is an application designed to help Go players enhance their skills through solving Tsumego problems. Tsumego are life-and-death problems in the game of Go, and solving them sharpens strategic thinking and problem-solving abilities.

There are many excellent Tsumego apps available online, such as GoProblems.com, Tsumego Hero, 101weiqi, and Go Magic. So, why create another one? Many existing apps often encourage a suboptimal practice: solving problems by trial and error. It's crucial to solve a problem by thoroughly reading it out, rather than just trying a move.

What sets Tsumego Solver apart is its unique approach. You can't play out the Tsumego problems here. Instead, you can only click 'solvable' or 'impossible'. This method fosters proper problem-solving techniques by requiring you to fully read and understand the solution before making a decision.

We require you to log in primarily to spot which problems are too easy or hard. As a bonus, logging in also allows you to show your progress over time.

We use Google accounts for login to avoid the hassle of managing passwords and to provide a secure, streamlined login process.

Most of the problems featured here are sourced from GoProblems.com. We are deeply grateful to them for their generous sharing policy.
